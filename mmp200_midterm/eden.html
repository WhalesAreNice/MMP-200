EDEN / The EDEN Project

Jonathon Ng is a 21 year old Irish electronic music producer and singer-singwriter based in Dublin Ireland. Formerly known as The EDEN Project, he now goes by a more simple name of EDEN.

It is still somewhat of a mystery as to why he changed his alias, but most speculate that it is a change in style of music or just to make it more simple.

From 2013-2015 he was under the alias of The EDEN Project and released many tracks on promotional networks. He released his first extended play "I Am Spitfire" in 2014, which was followed closely by his album Kairos.

He started to head towards a more indie-oriented alternative style, with his Bipolar Paradise EP, which included "Fumes" one of his more popular tracks. Soon after he declared that he will discontinue work as The EDEN Project with his last release called "Final Call".

In 2015-present, he came back as EDEN with his first album of his new alias "End Credits" being completely free to download worldwide.
