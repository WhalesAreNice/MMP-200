Bio

Jonathon Ng is a 21 year old Irish electronic music producer and singer-singwriter based in Dublin Ireland. Formerly known as The EDEN Project, he now goes by a more simple name of EDEN.

Born and raised in Dublin, Jonathon trained with classical violin and music theory from the age of 7. He later taught himself piano, guitar and drums, and began composing his own music.

He has since created his own record label, MCMXCV. Releasing music to the public for free and promoting himself through multiple platforms such as MonsterCat and NoCopyrightSounds.

His success has definitely been propelled by the growing EDM community. He now has his own concerts throughout the Europe and America.
