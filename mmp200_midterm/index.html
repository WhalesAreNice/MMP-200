EDEN

Jonathon Ng or better known as EDEN or EDEN Project is a producer and singer-songwriter from Dublin Ireland. His work is in the Electronic Dance Music genre, which he dabbles in dubstep, drum and bass, and more recently indie pop. With his growing popularity he is booked full of concerts throughout the year and you can buy a ticket to one of the concerts near you.
